How It Works

Mood tracking, made smart.

We use a sophisticated machine-learning algorithm trained to track the quantity of positive and negative thoughts you have on a regular basis, leveraging sentiment analysis to detect larger trends in your emotional state and mood cycles.

Download the mobile app

Download Positive.ly from the Google Play Store, and create an account to start dynamic tracking of your mood from your smartphone.

Continue using your phone

Use your smartphone as you usually would, and in the background Positive.ly will be working continuously with your mobile device to examine what you type on a day-to-day basis. This data helps inform our machine-learning model about your emotional state.

Check the website to track progress

View visual representations of how your emotion states vary as days, weeks, and months pass. This will help inform your own best practices and allow you to make changes to your lifestyle.

Use our emotional tools to take active steps

Use services such as Active Management and Gratitude Journal to better navigate your emotions and support yourself mentally.

Why It Works

Intelligent self-reflection, made possible.

According to a Yale study, increased emotional intelligence correlates with increased verbal and spatial intelligence, decreased anxiety and depression, and positive social functioning. Positive.ly helps you be aware of your feelings and emotions, discriminate among them, and use your newfound knowledge to take proactive steps toward a more positive mindset.

Smart Tracking

Positive.ly keeps tracks of your emotional fluctations and overall mood over the past 24 hours, as well as larger trends over the previous weeks and months. You can view your most positive thoughts from the past day, as well as how your mood changed chronologically.

Active Management

If Positive.ly detects consistently low mood ratings or signs of mental illness, it will alert you so you can seek help from primary care physicians and mental health professionals for proper diagnosis and treatment if necessary.

Gratitude Journal

Positive.ly has a built-in gratitude journal to promote a sense of gratefulness and enhance well-being by cultivating gratitude and directing it toward other people. Using a gratitude journal has been shown to improve mood balance and reduce symptoms of depression and anxiety in just one month.
